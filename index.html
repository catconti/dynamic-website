Hi, I'm Cat. I'm a front end developer.

A little bit about me.

I was born and raised in San Francisco, the city I will most likely call home for the rest of my days. My journey into tech started at the age of 8, through a love of a particular cartoon tv show. While I explored fan websites dedicated to Sailor Moon, I was inspired to learn basic HTML and create my own.

Years later, I created another website as a birthday gift.. And then another for my cake decorating hobby. More and more, I realized how much I loved to code, and have since learned best practices and industry standards of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This has allowed me to create things that make people smile, all while using concise and organized code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
